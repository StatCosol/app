# StatComply Module-Wise Trainer SOP

This document is a trainer-ready operating guide for onboarding and refresher sessions. Use it together with the screenshots in `docs/user-manual-assets` and the process flow document in `docs/MODULE_WISE_TRAINING_FLOW.md`.

## 1. Trainer Use

Use this sequence for every session:

1. Introduce the role and explain what the user is responsible for.
2. Show the dashboard and explain the left-side navigation.
3. Demonstrate the main work cycle in live screens.
4. Show one exception case such as rejection, overdue, or escalation.
5. Give the trainee a guided hands-on exercise.
6. Verify the trainee can explain the handoff to the next team.

## 2. Recommended Training Agenda

### 2.1 Induction Agenda

1. Platform overview and login flow: 10 minutes
2. Role dashboard and navigation: 10 minutes
3. Core process demonstration: 20 minutes
4. Exception and escalation handling: 10 minutes
5. Trainee practice: 15 minutes
6. Q and A and recap: 10 minutes

### 2.2 Full-Day Role Training Agenda

1. Portal introduction and role scope
2. Data flow and ownership boundaries
3. Primary transaction flow
4. Review and approval flow
5. Error, rejection, and escalation flow
6. Reporting and closure flow
7. Hands-on exercises
8. Assessment and sign-off

## 3. Common Trainer Script

Use these lines in every role session:

1. Ask the trainee to identify the dashboard cards and what they mean.
2. Ask the trainee to identify which menu is used daily and which menu is used only for exceptions.
3. Ask the trainee what happens if an item is rejected or overdue.
4. Ask the trainee who receives the work after their step is complete.

## 4. Admin Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to onboard a client, create users, assign owners, and monitor system health.

### 4.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the Admin dashboard.
2. Explain system KPI cards and the alert areas.
3. Open **Users** and demonstrate search, filter, activate, deactivate, and edit.
4. Expand **Create User** and create a sample role-based user.
5. Open **Clients** and demonstrate company creation.
6. Add or explain branch setup and compliance applicability.
7. Open **Assignments** and map CRM and Auditor.
8. Open **Payroll Assignments** and map Payroll.
9. Return to the dashboard and explain how Admin spots gaps before work starts.

### 4.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to explain which roles must be created for a new client.
2. Ask the trainee to identify where missing CRM, Auditor, or Payroll ownership is corrected.
3. Ask the trainee to explain what happens after the client is fully onboarded.

### 4.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can explain client onboarding start to finish.
2. Trainee can identify where assignments are completed.
3. Trainee can explain downstream handoff to Client, CRM, Auditor, and Payroll.

## 5. CEO Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to review executive risk, approvals, escalations, reports, and register outputs.

### 5.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the CEO dashboard.
2. Explain each KPI card and the executive guardrails.
3. Open **Approvals** and explain approval versus rejection handling.
4. Open **Escalations** and explain how critical items are reviewed.
5. Open **Reports** and show preview and export.
6. Open **Registers** and explain what executive users review there.

### 5.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to identify which items require immediate executive attention.
2. Ask the trainee to explain what happens to a rejected approval.
3. Ask the trainee to explain when a case should remain with CCO and when it should move to CEO attention.

### 5.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can use dashboard, approvals, and escalations together.
2. Trainee can explain how executive decisions flow back to operating teams.

## 6. CCO Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to monitor CRM performance, process approvals, manage controls, and drive escalation closure.

### 6.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the CCO dashboard.
2. Explain pending approvals, total CRMs, overdue tasks, and escalations.
3. Show CRM performance and top overdue portfolios.
4. Open **Approvals** and explain decision handling.
5. Open **Controls** and explain SLA thresholds and reminder logic.
6. Open **Escalations** and show assign, notify, and close actions.
7. Open **Registers** and explain central visibility.

### 6.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to identify which CRM needs intervention first.
2. Ask the trainee to explain how controls influence escalation behavior.
3. Ask the trainee to explain the upward handoff to CEO.

### 6.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can interpret CRM performance.
2. Trainee can explain approval, control, and escalation flow.

## 7. CRM Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to manage assigned clients, review branch submissions, process filings, and coordinate audits.

### 7.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the CRM dashboard.
2. Explain the action workbench and due queues.
3. Open **All Clients** and enter one client workspace.
4. Show how CRM checks branch compliance, documents, and client context.
5. Open **Document Review Center** and demonstrate approve versus reupload.
6. Open **Returns / Filings** and explain filing status progression.
7. Open **Audits** and show how an audit is created and assigned.
8. Open **SLA Tracker** and **Escalations** and explain when items move upward.

### 7.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to describe what CRM reviews before approving a branch submission.
2. Ask the trainee to explain the difference between a normal filing update and a reupload request.
3. Ask the trainee to explain who receives the work after CRM approval or rejection.

### 7.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can manage the daily review queue.
2. Trainee can explain review, returns, and audit coordination.

## 8. Client Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to monitor company compliance, branches, employees, approvals, and support actions.

### 8.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the Client dashboard.
2. Explain filters and KPI cards.
3. Open **Branches** and explain branch-level visibility.
4. Open **Employees** and explain employee master review.
5. Open **Approvals Center** and explain business approvals.
6. Open **My Queries** and explain support follow-up.

### 8.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to identify which branch requires attention.
2. Ask the trainee to explain where employee master corrections are reviewed.
3. Ask the trainee to explain when to raise a support query instead of waiting for CRM follow-up.

### 8.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can use company-level visibility for operational follow-up.
2. Trainee can explain handoff to Branch, Payroll, or CRM.

## 9. Branch Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to upload monthly and periodic compliance records, handle reuploads, and maintain branch compliance execution.

### 9.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the Branch dashboard.
2. Explain headcount, pending PF or ESIC, compliance score, and uploads.
3. Open **Monthly Compliance** and explain the workbench.
4. Select one item and demonstrate upload.
5. Open **Periodic Uploads** and explain quarterly, half-yearly, and yearly submissions.
6. Demonstrate how returned items are corrected and resubmitted.
7. Open **Helpdesk** and explain when to use it.

### 9.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to explain how they identify overdue items.
2. Ask the trainee to explain the difference between first submission and reupload.
3. Ask the trainee to explain where the uploaded item goes after submission.

### 9.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can complete upload work correctly.
2. Trainee can explain CRM review handoff and escalation if delayed.

## 10. Auditor Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to execute assigned audits, raise observations, request reuploads, and finalize reports.

### 10.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the Auditor dashboard.
2. Explain filters and audit counts.
3. Open **Audits** and select an assigned audit.
4. Enter the workspace and explain scope, evidence tray, and checklist.
5. Demonstrate requesting reupload for insufficient evidence.
6. Demonstrate observation creation and severity scoring.
7. Open **Reports** and explain report builder and export.

### 10.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to explain when reupload is required.
2. Ask the trainee to explain how observations move into the final report.
3. Ask the trainee to explain who consumes the completed audit output.

### 10.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can execute an audit workspace flow.
2. Trainee can explain audit report closure and downstream review.

## 11. Contractor Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to complete tasks, upload evidence, respond to rejection, and raise support queries.

### 11.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the Contractor dashboard.
2. Explain task summary cards and pending uploads.
3. Open **Tasks** and pick one task.
4. Demonstrate start, evidence upload, note entry, and submit.
5. Explain how rejected tasks are corrected and resubmitted.
6. Open **Support** and show query creation.

### 11.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to explain what must be checked before clicking submit.
2. Ask the trainee to explain what to do if the reviewer rejects the evidence.
3. Ask the trainee to explain when to use task comments and when to use support tickets.

### 11.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can complete a task flow without guidance.
2. Trainee can respond properly to reupload or rejection.

## 12. Payroll Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to work inside client-scoped payroll workspaces, process runs, and generate outputs.

### 12.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the Payroll dashboard.
2. Explain run, employee, PF, ESI, and register indicators.
3. Open **Clients** and enter a client workspace.
4. Explain why payroll operations are client-scoped.
5. Open **Payroll Runs** and explain the run sequence.
6. Demonstrate review of validation exceptions and run submission.
7. Open **Reports** and explain period-based output download.
8. Explain how published runs feed ESS and other outputs.

### 12.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to explain the payroll run stages in order.
2. Ask the trainee to explain where client-specific setup exists.
3. Ask the trainee to explain which outputs become visible after publishing.

### 12.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can navigate global versus client-scoped payroll pages.
2. Trainee can explain payroll processing and reporting flow end to end.

## 13. PF Team Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to triage and close PF, ESI, and payslip-related helpdesk tickets.

### 13.1 Demo Script

1. Log in and open the PF Team dashboard.
2. Explain total, open, in progress, and SLA-breached counts.
3. Open **Tickets** and apply filters.
4. Open one ticket and explain ticket history and resolution handling.
5. Demonstrate status update and closure.

### 13.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to identify which ticket must be handled first.
2. Ask the trainee to explain what information is needed before closure.
3. Ask the trainee to explain when Payroll coordination is needed.

### 13.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can triage tickets by urgency.
2. Trainee can explain resolution and closure flow.

## 14. ESS Trainer SOP

**Session goal:** the trainee should be able to use self-service for payslips, documents, leave, profile, and helpdesk.

### 14.1 Demo Script

1. Open the ESS login page and explain company code usage.
2. Log in and open the dashboard.
3. Explain latest payslip, PF, ESI, and leave balance cards.
4. Open **Payslips** and demonstrate download.
5. Open **Documents** and demonstrate preview and download.
6. Explain **My Profile**, **My PF**, **My ESI**, **Nominations**, **Leave**, and **Attendance**.
7. Open **Helpdesk** and explain issue raising.

### 14.2 Practice Exercise

1. Ask the trainee to download a payslip.
2. Ask the trainee to locate one document in the document vault.
3. Ask the trainee to explain where PF or payslip issues should be raised.

### 14.3 Expected Competency

1. Trainee can independently use ESS for routine self-service actions.
2. Trainee can explain which issues go to helpdesk.

## 15. Assessment Checklist

Use this checklist at the end of each module session:

1. Can the trainee explain the purpose of the role dashboard?
2. Can the trainee identify the main daily work queue?
3. Can the trainee explain what happens after approval?
4. Can the trainee explain what happens after rejection?
5. Can the trainee identify the next team in the process?
6. Can the trainee explain when escalation is required?
