# StatComply Module-Wise User Manual

Screenshots in this manual were captured from the local running application on 2026-03-29.

## Scope

This version covers the live modules that were verified with working accounts in the current environment:

- Admin
- CEO
- CCO
- CRM
- Client
- Branch
- Auditor
- Contractor
- Payroll
- PF Team
- ESS

## 1. Login Process

Use this process for every portal:

1. Open the application URL.
2. Enter your official email ID.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click **Sign In**.
5. Wait for the role dashboard to load.
6. Use the left menu to move between module pages.

![Login Screen](./user-manual-assets/login.png)

## 2. Admin Module

**Purpose:** control users, clients, assignments, governance, reports, payroll setup, and escalations.

![Admin Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/admin-dashboard.png)

![Admin Users](./user-manual-assets/admin-users.png)

![Admin Clients](./user-manual-assets/admin-clients.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with an Admin account.
2. Start on the dashboard to review totals, alerts, SLA items, and open escalations.
3. Open **Users** to create, edit, activate, deactivate, or reset users.
4. Open **Clients** to register a new client and maintain client records.
5. Open **Assignments** to map CRM and Auditor ownership to each client.
6. Open **Payroll Assignments** and payroll setup pages when payroll ownership or templates need updates.
7. Review **Reports**, **Audit Logs**, **Digest**, and **Notifications** for operational follow-up.
8. Use **Escalations** and **Unassigned Clients** to clear pending governance actions.

## 3. CEO Module

**Purpose:** monitor enterprise performance, review escalations, and inspect board-level registers and reports.

![CEO Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/ceo-dashboard.png)

![CEO Registers](./user-manual-assets/ceo-registers.png)

![CEO Reports](./user-manual-assets/ceo-reports.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the CEO account.
2. Review the dashboard first for top-level compliance and performance indicators.
3. Open **Approvals** to review items routed for executive decision.
4. Open **Escalations** to inspect critical issues across clients or functions.
5. Open **Oversight** to compare business-unit status and current risk posture.
6. Open **Registers** for structured review of major tracked records.
7. Open **Reports** when you need summary reporting for management review.

## 4. CCO Module

**Purpose:** supervise CRM teams, handle approvals, monitor escalations, and track control registers.

![CCO Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/cco-dashboard.png)

![CCO Escalations](./user-manual-assets/cco-escalations.png)

![CCO Registers](./user-manual-assets/cco-registers.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the CCO account.
2. Start from the dashboard to review team metrics and pending follow-ups.
3. Open **CRM Management** or **CRMs Under Me** to inspect assigned CRM resources.
4. Open **Approvals** to act on routed approval requests.
5. Open **Oversight** to review compliance performance across the portfolio.

## 5. CRM Module

**Purpose:** manage client operations, compliance tracking, returns, audits, helpdesk, and reporting.

![CRM Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/crm-dashboard.png)

![CRM Returns](./user-manual-assets/crm-returns.png)

![CRM Clients](./user-manual-assets/crm-clients.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the CRM account.
2. Review the dashboard for due items, overdue work, risk indicators, and action counters.
3. Open **Clients** to access client-specific records and branch information.
4. Open **Compliance Tracker** to review upcoming, open, submitted, and delayed items.
5. Open **Returns / Filings** to create, monitor, or update statutory return activity.
6. Open **Audits** to schedule audits and track audit execution.
7. Open **Helpdesk** to raise or respond to operational tickets.
8. Use **Reports**, **Calendar**, **SLA**, **Heatmap**, and **Risk Trend** for planning and follow-up.
9. Open **Escalations** when a compliance issue needs higher-level visibility.

## 6. Client Module

**Purpose:** provide the master client view for branches, contractors, compliance status, payroll monitoring, employees, registers, and approvals.

![Client Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/client-dashboard.png)

![Client Branches](./user-manual-assets/client-branches.png)

![Client Employees](./user-manual-assets/client-employees.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the Client master-user account.
2. Start on the dashboard to review compliance, payroll, employee, and branch status.
3. Open **Branches** to inspect branch records and drill into branch workspaces.
4. Open **Contractors** to review contractor records tied to the client.
5. Open **Compliance Status**, **Returns**, **Registrations**, and **Library** to monitor statutory progress.
6. Open **Payroll** to review payroll monitoring when that access is enabled for the account.
7. Open **Employees** to create, review, approve, or update employee records.
8. Open **Registers**, **Audits**, **Approvals**, **Queries**, and **Unit Documents** for operational follow-up.
9. Use **Calendar**, **Heatmap**, **SLA**, **Risk Trend**, and **Escalations** for planning and governance review.

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the branch user account.
2. Review the dashboard for branch health, pending work, and recent notifications.
3. Open **Compliance Monthly** to update monthly compliance tasks and status.

7. Open **Contractors** to inspect contractor records mapped to the branch.
8. Open **Documents**, **Reports**, and **Notifications** for reference and follow-up.
9. Use **Helpdesk** to raise support tickets when branch action is blocked.

## 10. Payroll Module

**Purpose:** review payroll workload, open assigned client workspaces, and manage payroll processing from client-specific pages.

![Payroll Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/payroll-dashboard.png)

![Payroll Clients](./user-manual-assets/payroll-clients.png)

![Payroll Reports](./user-manual-assets/payroll-reports.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the Payroll account.
2. Start on the dashboard to review assigned clients, active runs, and pending workload.
3. Open **Clients** to see the list of payroll accounts assigned to you.
4. Select a client from the client workspace.
5. Inside the selected client, open the required area such as **Overview**, **Employees**, **Runs**, **PF/ESI**, **Queries**, **Setup**, or **Registers**.
6. Complete payroll processing or review tasks inside that client-specific workspace.
7. Use **Reports**, **TDS Calculator**, and **Gratuity Calculator** when needed.

## 11. PF Team Module

**Purpose:** triage PF/ESI-related ticket queues and work ticket-level updates.

![PF Team Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/pf-dashboard.png)

![PF Team Tickets](./user-manual-assets/pf-tickets.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Log in with the PF Team account.
2. Review the dashboard for workload, counts, and current queue position.
3. Open **Tickets** to inspect the full PF/ESI ticket list.
4. Open a ticket to review details, attachments, and previous updates.
5. Change the ticket status or add progress notes based on the work completed.
6. Return to the dashboard to monitor pending and resolved items.

## 12. ESS Module

**Purpose:** allow employees to access self-service functions such as profile details, nominations, leave, payslips, PF/ESI, attendance, documents, and helpdesk.

![ESS Login](./user-manual-assets/ess-login.png)

![ESS Dashboard](./user-manual-assets/ess-dashboard.png)

![ESS Payslips](./user-manual-assets/ess-payslips.png)

![ESS Documents](./user-manual-assets/ess-documents.png)

### Step-by-step process

1. Open the ESS portal login page.
2. Enter the **Company Code** provided by HR or your employer.
3. Enter the employee email ID.
4. Enter the ESS password.
5. Click **Sign In** to open the employee dashboard.
6. After login, use the ESS menu to access **Profile**, **PF**, **ESI**, **Nominations**, **Leave**, **Payslips**, **Attendance**, **Documents**, and **Helpdesk**.
7. Review the dashboard first for leave balances, nomination status, payslip counts, and statutory information.
8. Open **Payslips** to view available salary slips and download files where available.
9. Open **Documents** for employee document history and vault access.
10. Open **Helpdesk** to raise PF, ESI, or payslip-related issues.

## 13. Recommended Daily Usage Pattern

Use this sequence for day-to-day operations:

1. Log in and review the dashboard first.
2. Open notifications or escalations next.
3. Complete the main transaction pages for your role.
4. Update status, remarks, or submissions before leaving a record.
5. Recheck dashboard counts to confirm the queue has reduced.
